The Manhole Infrastructure of Wellington

Citylink Manholes

These manholes are used as access points to the network of fibre optic cables around the CBD's of Wellington. This allows the company to perfrom maintainance on the cables and create exxpand the network.

Chorus Manholes

Chorus is another company like citylink that provides Wellington with access to the internet through their underground cabling systems. They use manholes of varying sizes to access and repair their network of cables

Wellington City Council Manholes

Council manholes can provide a similar function to that of Citylink and Chorus manholes however these manholes can be used to access the vast Wastewatwer and sewage network. This is crutial to tackle blockage problems and access cables.
