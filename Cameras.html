The Camera Infrastructure of Wellington

CCTV Camera

CCTV or Closed-circuit television is used as a means to monitor and record what happens in the streets by the government. This is in order to maintain peace through survaillance, as various actions of individuals will be able to be verified.

Private use CCTV

Many privately owned industries/businesses make use of this technology as a means of protection of their operations and locations. Potential theives are disuaded by the possibility of them being identified through the CCTV footage.

Traffic Camera

A traffic camera is a video camera which observes the vehicular traffic that occurs on the roads they are placed at. They play an important role in road safetly as it allows operator to remotely activate safety equipment and monitor traffic congestion.
